  • Martin Luther King Jr.: A central leader of the Civil Rights Movement, known for his activism and "I Have a Dream" speech.
  • Nelson Mandela: Led the fight against apartheid in South Africa and became its first Black president.
  • Harriet Tubman: Abolitionist who led hundreds of enslaved people to freedom via the Underground Railroad.
  • Malcolm X: A prominent, influential voice for Black empowerment and human rights.
